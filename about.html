Our Story

Driving Excellence Since 2009

At Rad Motors, we believe a car is more than metal and machinery, it is a symbol of progress, confidence, and personal success. Every vehicle tells a story, and we are proud to be part of our customers’ journeys. Founded on integrity, ambition, and an uncompromising standard for quality, Rad Motors was built to redefine the car-buying experience. We specialize in sourcing clean, premium foreign-used and locally used vehicles that combine performance, comfort, and long-term value. Every unit in our inventory is carefully selected, inspected, and presented with transparency. Our approach is simple yet powerful: Trust. Quality. Excellence. We understand that buying a car is not just a transaction, it is an investment. That is why we prioritize honesty in pricing, clarity in documentation, and professionalism in every interaction. From first inquiry to final handover, we ensure a smooth, confident, and rewarding experience. At Rad Motors, we don’t chase quick sales, we build lasting relationships. Our growing reputation is driven by satisfied clients who value reliability, class, and a dealership that stands behind its word. We are not just moving cars. We are delivering confidence, ambition, and the freedom to move forward

Our journey began with a passion for exceptional automobiles and a commitment to customer service that goes above and beyond. Over the past 15 years, we've helped thousands of customers find their perfect vehicles, from first-time luxury buyers to seasoned collectors.
